About Pride Park

Everyone has a story to tell. Some of us want to share theirs, some want to witness other's. Whether you feel the need to make your voice heard, get something of your chest or find out that there are other people like you, this site wants to help you feel more connected with other members of the LGBTQ+ community anywhere.

By writing or recording a little insight into your life and sharing it with others, you can offer support, give advice or information or simply give someone a good laugh and make them relate.

Many of us may feel involuntarily isolated by their identity. Being able to take part in somebody else's moments can show us that we are not alone in this and that we shouldn't be either.


So explore the little gems people from all walks of life have left here. Have a listen or a read and feel more connected.

Or share what you think is worth sharing and please be considerate when doing so. Should you feel your story may contain elements that could upset other people, make sure you include the Trigger Warning flag in your entry's tag. For a list of trigger warnings see the Blog page or Podcast page. Use those according to your best judgement (including subjects not covered by our list) and always be kind and respectful.


But most importantly - remember that you are valid!
