Share your Story!

Get out the recording device of your choice and hit the mic. Your story is worth sharing.
Keep it short and snappy with 10 minutes. However, nothing is stopping you from submitting more than one story. You can simply upload your file below.
Make sure you tag your story with the appropriate labels to make it easy for others to find the story that's right for them.

Please refer to our list of to our list of trigger warnings below and make sure you tag your entry in case it contains any of the following:

  • Abusive language
  • Depression
  • Suicide
  • Dysphoria
  • Mental illness
  • Mention of violence
  • Mention of verbal or physical abuse
